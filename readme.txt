Space Wars

Description: In this simple game, your goal as a player is to destroy as many enemy spaceships as possible. You have 3 lives and every enemy ship that reaches your side without being destroyed will result in you losing a life.

Controls: UP ARROW - move your ship up, DOWN ARROW - move your ship down, LEFT ARROW - move your ship left, RIGHT ARROW - move your ship right, Z - shoot bullets, ENTER - see instructions on game screen
